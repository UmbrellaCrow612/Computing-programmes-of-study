- **Relational Databases**
    
    - Tables, records, fields
        
    - Primary keys and foreign keys
        
    - Benefits: reduced redundancy, data consistency, relationships
        
- **Data Types**
    
    - Integer, String/Text, Boolean, Real/Float/Decimal, Date
        
- **SQL Queries**
    
    - `SELECT` (with specific columns, `WHERE`, `ORDER BY`)
        
    - `DELETE FROM` with `WHERE` clause
        
    - Joining tables implicitly via foreign keys
